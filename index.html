Laurindo Feliciano's The Possible Book

Welcome to the Virtual RMMS Library version of the celebrated Brazillian artist Laurindo Feliciano's brand-new coloring book The Possible Book! Mr. Feliciano generously made the coloring book available for free for everyone to access. Now it has been transformed into an online version by a super programmer Erina Chavez (Ms. Chavez's daughter!) over a Zoom session one evening to make it possible for all of you to color. Enjoy!!!

  1. Choose your image from the choices on the right.
  2. Your cursor will become a paintbrush when you click and drag within the canvas.
  3. Choose a color from the palette by clicking the color you want.
  4. You can change the brush width by toggling the Paint Brush Size slider.
  5. Once you are done coloring, click on the "Save" button on the bottom left, then click "Show in Folder"
  6. Attach your coloring sheet file "The Possible Book" from your Drive, and share with Ms. Chavez!
